Music Theory

🎵 Notes

Music notes are sounds with different pitches. Main notes are A, B, C, D, E, F and G.

🎼 Scale

A scale is a group of notes arranged in order. Example: C major scale is C D E F G A B C.

🥁 Rhythm

Rhythm is the pattern of beats in music. It controls the timing and movement of a song.

🎶 Melody

Melody is a sequence of musical notes that sounds pleasant and forms the main tune.

Musical Instruments

🎸 Guitar

A string instrument played by strumming or plucking. Used in rock, pop and classical music.

🎹 Piano

A keyboard instrument with black and white keys. It is useful for learning melody and harmony.

🥁 Drums

A percussion instrument used to create beats, rhythm and energy in music.

🎻 Violin

A string instrument played with a bow. It is common in classical and orchestral music.
